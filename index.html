  1. Periods of absence from duty on the grounds of ill health must be supported by a medical certificate signed by a duly registered medical practitioner.
  2. The certificate should be submitted within five (5) working days from the first day of absence and should cover the entire period of absence.
  3. Where medical certificates are submitted in support of applications for leave of absence on the grounds of illness, officers are required to ensure that the certificates (whether issued in Jamaica or elsewhere) contain the following information:
  1. Date of issue
  2. Date of onset of illness
  3. A statement as to whether or not the officer is unable to perform his duties because of the nature of his illness and also the likely duration of such illness.
